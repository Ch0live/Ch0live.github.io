15th to 17th October

Visiting the Komodo Dragons

This time I'm writing to you about my time in East Nusa Tenggara. As Indonesia was the final country I visited before flying out of Asia I wanted to make sure I did as many things as possible while here that can't be done elsewhere in the world. For me, this meant seeing the Komodo Dragons in their natural habitat. I add that last detail on as I found out on the tour there are actually 2 of these lizards in Colchester. Take note that if you'd like to see these animals and don't want to fly halfway around the world to do so, there are in fact some just a few hours from London.
